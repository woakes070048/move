About

MOVE was designed and developed in 2014 to help with vaccine delivery operations in northern Nigeria.

What is it?

MOVE is a Logistic Management Information System. It has supply chain functions that facilitate the efficient delivery of essential  medicines or any other stock items to last-mile health facilities.

Who uses it?

Public health workers use MOVE because it helps them with consistent medical supply chain reporting and provides prompt information on stock levels. 

Where is it used?

It is (currently) deployed in 123 wards across 19 LGAs of Kano State.
