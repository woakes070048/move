Features

MOVE offers many options to make your medical supply chain efficient

Stock Count

Users are guided through the stock count process with intelligent UI prompts, driven by reminders from facility-specific schedules and inventories.

Waste Count

Report vaccine wastage and detail the specific type of wastage (broken vial, VVM stage 3, opened expiry, etc) as it happens.

Stock Out

Report stock out, broadcast to relevant supply chain officers for rapid response. If your internet connection is poor or non-existent, these alerts are seamlessly transmitted over SMS.

CCE Breakdown

Report Cold Chain Equipment breakdown, include the type and serial number of the equipment.

Delivery

Log incoming and outgoing ledger entries along with dynamic batch, expiry date and VVM registration.

manage your data

Granular Analysis

View aggregated MOVE reports in charts. Your data is grouped into zones to show you the big picture, and you can drill down to more granular levels.

Export Data

Export reports from each admin level - State, Zone, LGA, Health Facility. Reports are exported as Excel (.xls) files.
